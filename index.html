Welcome to our Kindergarten Readiness Flash Card site! Where you can play different kinds of flash cards to help get you ready for your first year in kindergarten.

Instructions: Cards will have counting and identification.

Counting Objects, you will only need to click the correct number key for the number of objects on the card.
Identifying Objects, you will only need to click the key of the first letter of the object.
